brief intruction

Data annotation is an important part of deep learning research and development. However, the existing annotation platforms have shortcomings such as low efficiency, excessive labor consumption and too long annotation cycle. FastLabel, as the name suggests, is an intelligent label that makes it easy for users to quickly get the label results. Its intelligence is mainly reflected in ai-assisted annotation, while its rapidity is reflected in smooth interactive experience.
