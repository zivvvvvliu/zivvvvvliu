Game Design

brief intruction

This is a small game made by processing, players can click the mouse to generate a new square, layer by layer, to see who can stack the highest square.



Game mechanics

At the beginning of the game, a square will move left and right. Players can click the left mouse button to make the square stop moving.

If the stopped moving block exceeds the previous block, it will be cut off and a new block will be generated. The length of the new block will be the same as that of the cut block.

Stack one layer on top of another until the moving square has no overlap with the previous one, and the game ends.
