brief intruction

The scene is set in a chamber of secrets, the elves want to play tricks on the player, the player needs to find all four elves to go out of the chamber of secrets. The chamber is decorated with various props can start organ, players need to help themselves through the mobile phone AR. After solving the puzzle, the corresponding elf will appear. Hints for the puzzle are included in each elf's character description, which is posted in the chamber of secrets. As long as the space of four elves have appeared, the door of the meeting room will open.

user research

As can be seen from the information in the chart: There are still many shortcomings in the existing escape rooms, and the traditional way is not easy to solve, the introduction of AR may be a good choice. Secondly, users who are keen on traditional room escape are also very interested in adding AR to the room escape.
