brief intruction

Will printing be so much of an inconvenience in three to five years? Just like ele.me made ordering food by phone a thing of the past, didi made it unnecessary for people to hail a taxi. After the technology gradually matures, does the gap between stores still exist? Could printing be easier, too?

And what will be the future of print shops?



PREVIOUS RESEARCH

user research

We interviewed five representative print shops around the school and recorded them.

the problem

1. Low communication efficiency

2. Lack of promotion

3. The energy of network business is insufficient

user research

We interviewed and recorded the teachers, classmates and employees we knew. We also issued questionnaires and collected 76 copies.

Design definition

Based on the above research results, we envision that the future print shop should be a platform software connecting all print shops in the form of the Internet of things.
