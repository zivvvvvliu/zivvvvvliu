brief intruction

Shan Hai Jing, or the Classic of Mountains and Seas, is a classic Chinese text compiled of mythical geography and figures. Readers over the past hundreds of years have been both baffled and amazed by the imaginary world it creates. According to studies of Shan Hai Jing, the writer of the book got the inspirations of the stories from various old paintings.

I want to use interactive media to help modern people relive the world of Shan Hai Jing, a world where fiction and reality intertwines. I want to projectors and “shadow play” to show four kinds of beasts from Shan Hai Jing, then use interactive technology to enable the audience to interact with the beasts.



Prototype & Interactions

Composition: "mountains" paperboard model + projection

Technique: We used Arduino for hardware and Procesing for software

Arduino

The Arduino is responsible for controlling all the components in the physical model and sending the information to the remote computer through the wireless module after receiving the information detected by the person approaching

2.4 G wi-fi module

Because the model is compared with the actual distance of the projection, wireless communication is required.

Processing

The wireless transmission module receives the signal through another wireless receiver and transfers it to Arduino for processing. Processing is responsible for controlling the content in the projection.
